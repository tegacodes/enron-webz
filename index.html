Welcome to the Enron Email Simulator.

Sign up for the unique opportunity to experience the the Enron Scandal from the inside. You will receive all 500,000 emails confiscated from Enron by the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission. Your expert guide is email and spam historian Dr Finn Brunton who has recorded a short welcome video and intermission lecture on the significance of the Enron corpus.
